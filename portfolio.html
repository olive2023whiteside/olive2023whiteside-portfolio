Whiteside's First Website

My go at coding was in building a website for a 30MM film project. The film projects was a personal diary from 4 NYUAD students recounting how they feel at the start of school. It was meant to caputre how attitudes and perspectives change from Freshman to Junior year.

In building a site for this projects, I aimed to create a digital and interactive poster-themed experience. Firsts are daunting and bring a bunch of frusteration, this coding project was no different, but I'm happy to present where I first started.

Sounds Like: A Meditation

Let's get zen! For the sound project, we chose to script and record a meditation. The concept of the audio was to use outdoor water sounds and instrumental music tones to create a full and immersive environment while using slow self-recorded audio as the meditation.

In building a website to house our project, we chose to draw thematic inspiration from the well established visual and audio iconography of meditation and restorative spa spaces which includes flower imagery and sleek, minimalist design. We were striving to create a relaxing experience which necessitated the creation of a low-distraction, easy-to-use site with strong continuity.
